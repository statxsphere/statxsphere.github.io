My Story

My data journey started with sports. As someone who was blown away by moments of magic created in various arenas as a kid, teenager, and even as an adult, I soon realised that I had a strong desire to be involved in this industry. Being struck by this realisation during my college days drove me to find my first internship in 2018. To this day, I am grateful to my time there, for not only helping me understand how sports businesses function but also for getting me hooked on the world of data science.

With minimal experience, maximal ambition, and perhaps a small nudge from being an MBA student, I co-founded my own sports analytics firm in 2019 as I believed that there were gaps that could be bridged in the sports analytics landscape in India. With a dream to take India to the FIFA World Cup fueling us, we were able to kick things off and run smoothly despite being a bootstrap venture. However, during the COVID-19 pandemic, we had to shut down as it is hard to analyze sports when no sports are being played. This was a very challenging time for me, but also a period of exponential growth where I channelled the shortcomings I faced and levelled up my skills to match my ambition and passion.

The impact of the pandemic ran deeper than just my skillset, however, as it ilicited within me a feeling of dread regarding climate change as another looming threat to civilization as we know it. Since 2021, I have actively and intentionally postioned myself as a stakeholder in the climate tech space, with a special focus on solar energy technology and carbon emissions monitoring as these are the domains where my interests and potential for impact intersect the most.

Today I see myself as a data and product professional vying for growth in role and responsibility, to be able to engage with stakeholders and affect change in order to leave the world a better place when I go out than when I came in. I hope we're able to help each other do the same. Cheers!
